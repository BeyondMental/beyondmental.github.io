About Me

With programming skills that have developed for over a decade, Aidan found a passion for game development that keeps on growing. He first got his start in 2015, using a copy of GameMaker 8.1 to develop simple games, and since then, has expanded his knowledge to learn multiple programming languages and game engines.

Aidan has Microsoft certifications in Java, JavaScript, and C# programming languages, with proficiency in Python, C++, Lua, and R. Aidan has developed games using GameMaker (GML & C++), Unity (C#), Unreal (Blueprints), and Godot (GDScript and C#). He has an additional passion for low-level programming languages, with hobbies in Basic and Assembly.

One of Aidan's recent projects, Chromaspectra, was recently showcased to the public at Hashtag Con x Indie Series.

Aidan is working toward a BFA in Computer Game Design at George Mason University. He currently works with Hashtag Gaming Arena, assisting the production of their collegiate eSports league, Beltway Battles.
