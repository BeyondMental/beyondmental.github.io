About Newton's Delivery Service

Features

Gravity-Manipulating Player Controller

The player character of Newton's Delivery Service utilizes an internal physics engine, allowing for gravity to be flipped upside down at a button's press! All of the players other actions, including jumping, sliding, wallrunning, and even a grappling hook, all work in both gravities. You can even reverse gravity mid action!

Speedrun Focused Levels

The levels in Newton's Delivery Service are designed to be finished in a minute or two, encouraging players to try again and improve their time. Each "district" of the city has unique obstacles and mechanics, it is up to you to learn their nuances and overcome their challenges.

Leaderboards

Once again encouraging players to go fast, climb the game's leaderboards and race to be the number one employee of Newton's Delivery Service! Anti-cheat was developed to track the player's movement, and invalidate all attempts that are physically impossible. These leaderboards are currently hosted on Unity Gaming Services.

Online Multiplayer

What's better then racing against the clock? Racing against your peers! Compete to see who is the fastest delivery driver while randomly chosen effects shake up the competition
