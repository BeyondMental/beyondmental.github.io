My Contributions

The M.I.X. Machine

In order to serve quality beverages to the customer, you'll have to make them first! Introducing the M.I.X. Machine! Simply flip levers, press buttons, and give it a nice smack to dispense a hot or cold beverage in seconds! Every unique ingredient adds a unique binary number to the total, making all 127 possible combinations on the menu! Data from the M.I.X. Machine is then transferred to cups using a particle collision.

Kitchen Gun!

Clean up your surroundings using the patented Kitchen Gun! Shoot dirty dishes and messes around the cafe to clean them up in a cloud of soapy bubbles! Depending on the object type, objects hit with the soap balls are either reset to their starting position or deleted. Clean up messes to keep your score and customer satisfaction high!

Weightless Objects

If an object is not interacted with after a prolonged random period of time, it will lose it's artificial gravity and slowly start to float away. Objects without gravity will regain regular physics after being shot with the Kitchen Gun or touching them with VR hands. This effect uses Unity's rigidbody and constant force systems.
